# How Social Norms Emerge

The formation of shared expectations.

This article folder follows the Sustainable Catalyst article-level computational scaffold. It may include conceptual models, synthetic data, calculator scripts, notebooks, documentation, tests, and multilingual examples that support the corresponding WordPress article.

## Folder layers

- `calculators/` — self-contained educational calculators and smoke tests.
- `data/` — synthetic or documented demonstration datasets.
- `docs/` — methods notes, data dictionaries, ethics notes, and responsible-use guidance.
- `outputs/` — generated tables, figures, and calculator outputs.
- language folders — Python, R, Julia, SQL, Haskell, Rust, Go, C, C++, Fortran, Java, TypeScript, and Prolog examples where appropriate.

## Responsible use

Examples are educational scaffolds only. They are not validated behavioral, clinical, financial, legal, policy, or operational tools.
